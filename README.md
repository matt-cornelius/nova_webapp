# Dono

Dono is a modern web application built with [your tech stack] that makes it radically easier for people to discover, share, and donate to charities, nonprofits, and other mission driven organizations.

## Install
Download and Run: build/windows/x64/runner/Release/hackathon_web_app.exe

## About

Dono is a social donation platform that removes friction from giving. Instead of hunting down individual donation pages and re entering payment information every time, users can discover causes, see what their network is supporting, and donate in just a few clicks from one connected account.

The app combines a familiar social feed with personalized recommendations and an AI powered search experience so people can find and support organizations that match their values and lived experiences.

## Features

* Social home feed showing donation activity from friends, family, and public figures in your network
* Rich donation posts that show who donated, how much, to which organization, plus captions and optional photos that explain why they donated
* Social engagement on donation posts including likes, comments, reposts, and one tap donate to the same cause
* Organization profile pages with mission, goals, who they serve, how funds are used, and other context the organization wants to share
* Personalized explore tab that surfaces donation activity outside your network based on your interests and past giving behavior
* Community goals and progress indicators for organizations working toward specific donation targets
* Dedicated donate tab with
  * History of organizations you and your network have supported
  * Search experience backed by an AI agent that finds relevant organizations for natural language queries such as “organizations that give to kids with cancer”
* Seamless donation flow where payment is handled through a connected account so giving to any supported organization takes only a few clicks
* Secure storage of user and payment data using environment based configuration
